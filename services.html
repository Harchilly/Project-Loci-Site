Our Solutions

Our Integrative Framework

We apply a comprehensive framework to every project. This structured approach ensures our solutions are effective, measurable, and scalable.

Our framework is based on four key pillars:

  • Familiarization of three levels: Guiding users through a space from a macro (overall layout), normal (sections/departments), and micro (tools and controls) perspective.
  • Pathway Runs/Training: Utilizing "ghost" runs and active runs with variability drills to lock in memory.
  • Advanced User Analytics: Integrating features to monitor and analyze user interactions, providing insights to optimize memory retention and spatial learning.
  • Data Feedback: Logging every attempt to provide continuous improvement and ROI tracking.

Familiarization: Macro Level

We start with a bird's-eye view of the entire facility. This helps learners orient themselves and understand the relationship between different departments, such as labs, packaging areas, and storage. Mnemonic characters are placed to help users instantly associate key information with a specific location.

By visualizing the space from above, learners can form a mental map of the facility before exploring the details of each section. This macro-level overview allows for better spatial memory, faster orientation, and improved navigation when engaging with real-world or simulated tasks.

Familiarization: Normal Level

Next, we guide users through the space at eye level. By placing our mnemonic characters and visual cues within the digital twin, we create a memorable narrative that turns a complex physical space into a "memory palace," making it easy to recall processes and pathways.

At this level, learners focus on the layout of each department, the positioning of equipment, and key interactions within the workspace. Using spatial storytelling, we anchor important procedures and safety steps in a visual context, making them easier to retrieve under pressure.

Familiarization: Micro Level

Finally, our mnemonics work at the most granular level. We focus on specific tools and controls, turning them into memorable characters. This helps users master the details critical for safety and operational efficiency, ensuring that even the smallest steps are retained for long-term recall.

Micro-level familiarization enables learners to understand and remember sequences of operations, the function of individual instruments, and subtle procedural differences. By embedding these details into a visual-spatial framework, the platform enhances precision, reduces errors, and accelerates skill mastery.
