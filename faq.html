Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

About Project Loci

Project Loci is a commercial startup specializing in immersive training and spatial learning. We use digital twin technology and the Method of Loci (MoL) to help organizations and individuals improve memory, knowledge retention, and training.

Our solution is an interactive, immersive platform built on a digital twin. It allows users to train in a realistic environment, offering a dynamic and engaging experience that goes far beyond traditional methods. By leveraging the power of spatial memory, our platform makes complex information easier to retain, ensuring that critical knowledge is always accessible when it matters most.

"Loci" is Latin for "places." It refers to the Method of Loci (MoL), a memory-enhancing technique that uses visualization and spatial memory to strengthen learning and recall. This is the core scientific principle behind all our training programs.

The Science Behind Our Method

MoL is a mnemonic strategy where a person visualizes a familiar physical space and mentally places items to be remembered within that space. To recall the information, they mentally "walk through" the space, retrieving the items. It is a well-established and research-supported technique.

Yes. Our team has conducted a systematic review on the use of MoL to mitigate cognitive decline. The research confirms that MoL is a promising and effective strategy for memory enhancement. Our approach is grounded in scientific literature and the principles of neuroscience.

Our Services and Benefits

A digital twin is a high-fidelity, 3D digital replica of a physical environment. We use this technology to create immersive and interactive training spaces

We combine the Method of Loci with digital twin technology to create custom training programs. Users can navigate a virtual space and interact with mnemonic anchors placed strategically within the digital environment. This helps them learn and recall complex information, such as safety protocols, maintenance routines, or emergency procedures, under realistic conditions.

Our services can be applied across a wide range of industries, including emergency response, maintenance and operations, security and onboarding, and education and learning.

The timeline for our training solutions is entirely client-dependent. It varies based on the scope of the project, including the size and complexity of the environment being scanned, as well as the depth of the training content required.

The process begins with a consultation to understand your specific training needs. We then use high-precision scanners to create a Digital Twin of your space. Our team works with you to integrate your SOPs and training materials into an immersive, science-backed learning experience that is fully customized to your goals.

We offer a pilot study as a proof of concept. This allows us to demonstrate the effectiveness of our immersive training solution for your specific use case, providing a clear understanding of its value before a full-scale deployment.

Our solution is designed to decrease the time it takes to achieve proficiency and reduce reliance on traditional classroom and shadowing hours. By leveraging the power of spatial memory, we create highly effective training that helps your team get up to speed faster.

While our primary focus is on providing immersive training solutions for organizations, we are developing resources and educational content to help individuals learn and apply the Method of Loci. Our blog/knowledge hub will be a great resource for this purpose.
