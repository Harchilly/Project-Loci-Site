Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

Project Loci is a free tutoring initiative by students from Western University, aimed at helping students in grades 6-12 excel in their studies using the Method of Loci—a scientifically proven memory technique that enhances retention and recall.

The Method of Loci is a powerful memory technique that involves visualizing information in specific locations (like rooms in a house) to improve memory and learning. It’s an effective and fun way to retain academic concepts.

Our tutors are knowledgeable university students experienced in using and teaching the Method of Loci. They are passionate about education and dedicated to helping students improve their academic skills.

Services Offered

We provide tutoring across a diverse range of subjects, including Chemistry, Biology, History, Psychology, and other humanities disciplines.
